# EcoSapien

**AI-powered solutions for sustainable environmental impact.**

EcoSapien develops intelligent systems that enable organizations to make data-driven decisions for sustainability across agriculture, waste management, and resource optimization.

[www.ecosapien.de](https://www.ecosapien.de)

## Current Focus

### Sustainable Dairy Operations & Environmental Impact

Research and optimization of dairy operations with a focus on sustainable practices and environmental stewardship — monitoring 100+ head of cattle across 8 breeds using AI-driven analytics.

**Key research areas:**

- **Milk Yield Optimization** — Analyzing production across breeds in response to organic fodder, vitamin supplementation, and nutritional protocols
- **Animal Health Monitoring** — Real-time health surveillance with early intervention and productivity correlation
- **Milk Quality Analysis** — Compositional analysis of fat content, protein levels, somatic cell counts, and nutritional profiles
- **Genomic Tracking & Selection** — AI-assisted breeding optimization through progeny genomic tracking
- **Environmental Impact Assessment** — Monitoring sanitization, sewage treatment, and soil quality impact
- **AI-Powered Expansion Planning** — ML-driven identification of optimal locations for sustainable growth
- **Sustainable Resource Management** — Data-driven feed optimization, water conservation, and waste reduction
